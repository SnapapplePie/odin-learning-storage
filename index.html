Flexboxes

What are flexboxes?

Flexboxes help us organise information into rows or columns. They will grow or shrink (aka flex) based on defined rules. Flex containers are any elgiement with the property:
display: flex
on it. A flex item is anything INSIDE a flex container. Flex containers can also be flex items, allowing the flex item to contain its own flex items, or children.
